Basic counting program (to 100):

INS 100 3 --Loads 100 into register 3
INS 1 2 --Loads 1 into register 2
ADD 1 2 1 --Adds register 1 and 2, sending the result back to register 1
OUT 1 --Sends register 1 to the output of the CPU
JMPC 3 > 1 2 --checks if register 1 is less than 100, looping back to instruction 2 (the addition).

Basic counting program (endless):

INS 1 2 --Loads 1 into register 2
ADD 1 2 1 --Adds register 1 and register 2, sending the result back to register 1
OUT 1 --Sends register 1 to the output of the CPU
JMP 1 --Jumps back to instruction 1 (addition)
